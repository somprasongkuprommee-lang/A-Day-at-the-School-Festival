📖 Reading Material: A Day at the School Festival

Every year, our school has a school festival. Students and teachers prepare many fun activities for everyone. The festival is usually held on Saturday.

In the morning, students decorate their classrooms and set up booths. Some students sell food and drinks such as noodles, snacks, and juice. Other students prepare games and performances.

In the afternoon, many visitors come to the school. Parents, friends, and neighbors join the festival. They enjoy the food, play games, and watch shows.

One popular activity is the talent show. Some students sing songs, some dance, and others play musical instruments. The audience claps loudly and cheers for them. It brings everyone together and creates happy memories.
